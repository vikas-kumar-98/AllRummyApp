Basic Rummy Rules

Rummy is a classic card game where the objective is to form valid sets and sequences with the cards you're dealt.

  • Each player is dealt 13 cards
  • Form valid sequences and sets
  • One pure sequence is mandatory
  • Discard one card after each turn
  • First to declare with valid sets wins

1

Deal the Cards

Each player is dealt 13 cards. The remaining cards form the closed deck, and one card is placed face-up to start the open deck.

2

Form Sets & Sequences

Arrange your cards into valid sequences (3+ consecutive cards of same suit) and sets (3-4 cards of same rank, different suits).

3

Draw and Discard

On your turn, draw a card from either deck, then discard one card to the open deck. Aim to complete your sets.

4

Declare When Ready

When you have formed at least two sequences (one pure) and all cards arranged in sets, declare to win the game.
