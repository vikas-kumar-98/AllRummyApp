How to Play Rummy

Basic Rules

Rummy is a card game where the objective is to form valid sets and sequences. A valid declaration must include at least two sequences, one of which must be a pure sequence (without a joker).

Pure Sequence

A consecutive sequence of 3 or more cards of the same suit without any joker.

Impure Sequence

A consecutive sequence of 3 or more cards of the same suit with jokers.

Sets

3 or 4 cards of the same rank but different suits.
