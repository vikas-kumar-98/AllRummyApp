Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about online rummy and our recommended apps

Is playing rummy online legal in India?

Yes, playing rummy online for cash is legal in India as it has been classified as a game of skill by the Supreme Court. However, laws may vary by state, so it's advisable to check your local regulations. All apps we recommend operate within the legal framework and have necessary certifications.

How do I withdraw my winnings?

Withdrawal processes vary by platform but generally involve these steps:

  1. Complete your account verification (KYC process)
  2. Navigate to the cashier or withdrawal section
  3. Select your preferred withdrawal method (UPI, bank transfer, etc.)
  4. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw
  5. Confirm the transaction

Most apps process withdrawals within 24-48 hours, with some offering instant withdrawals for verified users.
